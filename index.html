About Me

Hi, my name is Allen! I am a sophomore studying Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin with a strong interest in hardware design, electronics, and embedded systems. I enjoy working across the full hardware development lifecycle, from design and optimization to testing and verification. I also enjoy collaborating in team environments to streamline development and contribute to a positive community, and I am committed to expanding my knowledge and developing my technical skills to create impact through real-world applications.

I am currently involved in University of Texas at Austin Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers Robotics and Automation Society (UT IEEE RAS) as an officer and the fundraising coordinator. UT IEEE RAS is one of UT Austin's largest robotics organizations, and we are dedicated to making robotics and robotics education more accessible for all. Consider donating to support our mission and our projects!
