I.D.C.I

10/2024

Overview

An ID card device to improve communication and enhance sensory feedback for nurses. Designed to more easily page emergency codes at hospital rooms and to indicate an active codes to stand-by nurses by displaying the specific code and the room in need of assistance, and create vibration using a mini servo to mitigate sensory fatigue. Programmed using an Arduino Nano. Won 2nd place out of 17 competing teams in the 2024 SEC Makeathon.
