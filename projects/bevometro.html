BevoMetro

03/2025 - 04/2025

Overview

An autonomous tram that travels around the UT campus for an efficient, convenient, and accessible form of transporation. Powered by an ESP32, the tram travels along an established path that loops around the UT campus using an infrared sensor that detects the path. The tram also detects and stops for obstructions, pedestratians, and its routine stations using an sonar sensor, and an LED lights indicates whether it is moving, stopping at a station, or stopping for an obstruction or a pedestrian. Won "Community Favorite Award" in the 2025 Project Ramshorn Showcase.
