Buck Converter

03/2025 - 04/2025

Overview

A 20V to 5V switching buck converter board developed for labs 11, 12, and 13 of ECE 302, Intro to Electrical Engineering, using an inductor, dual op-amp, MOSFETs, and a MOSFET gate driver to regulate the input voltage with pulse-width modulation and create a voltage step-down for the output. Designed with KiCad.
