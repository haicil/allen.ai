SmartTrak Wallet

Overview

A Bluetooth smart wallet that tracks proximity distance to a connected device and allow wireless controls to help users secure its position. Designed to solve situations with wallets gone missing or misplaced by helping the user see the distance between them and their wallet, and provide sensory signals of light and audio to help them find their wallet. Programmed using an ESP32. Presented as a capstone project in Stevenson High School's Engineering Design and Development.
